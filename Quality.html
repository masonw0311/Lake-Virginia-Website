Lakeside Neighborhood at Rollins College

The Lakeside Neighborhood at Rollins College lies on the banks of Lake Virginia and revolutonizes the college's mission of a revolutionary liberal education by prioritizing a balance between academics and personal well-being.

All sorts of students have popularized Lakeside for it's breathtaking views of the lake, making it popular for study sessions in the lounge, workouts in the fitness center, swims at the pool, and a bite or drink from the cafe.
