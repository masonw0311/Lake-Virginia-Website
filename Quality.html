It would come as no surprise that living near or on a lake would be one of the most satisfying feelings on earth, and Lake Virginia is no exception. Find out why living on Lake Virginia is one of the best feelings in Central Florida!

Lakeside Neighborhood at Rollins College

The Lakeside Neighborhood at Rollins College lies on the banks of Lake Virginia and revolutionizes the college's mission by prioritizing a balance between academics and personal well-being.

Students love Lakeside for its breathtaking views of the lake, popular study sessions in the lounge, workouts in the fitness center, swims at the pool, and a bite or drink from the cafe.

Mental Health Benefits

Living on a lake improves mental health and wellness by promoting relaxation and reducing stress. The serene environment of Lake Virginia creates a sense of peace, boosting emotional well-being and productivity.

On Lake Virginia, students and residents alike can find calm and motivation to tackle any challenges they face.
