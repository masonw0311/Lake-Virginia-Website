About Lake Virginia

Lake Virginia is one of the beautiful lakes in the Winter Park Chain of Lakes, located in the heart of Winter Park, Florida. Known for its serene waters, scenic views, and rich ecosystem, Lake Virginia offers a variety of recreational activities, including boating, kayaking, and fishing.

The lake is bordered by Rollins College, whose picturesque campus enhances the lake’s charm, and it provides a unique outdoor setting for students and visitors alike. With lush parks, walking paths, and historic homes lining its shores, Lake Virginia is a peaceful retreat where nature and community meet.

It's a popular destination for those looking to enjoy Florida’s natural beauty and is also home to numerous species of fish, birds, and other wildlife, making it a haven for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers.
